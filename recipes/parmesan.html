Parmesan

Description

You’ve just found the crispiest, most restaurant-worthy Chicken Parmesan recipe out there! In this recipe, I use some chef-inspired techniques combined with years of my own chicken parmesan-making experience to create the best dish that you and your family will love!

Ingredients

  • 2 large boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • ¼ cup vegetable oil
  • ½ cup flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 ½ cups panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 cup marinara sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425°
  2. Combine the flour, egg, and breading mixtures in 3 separate shallow pans
  3. Slice the chicken in half lengthwise to create 2-3 thinner slices
  4. Dredge completely in the flour mixture
  5. Dip the chicken in the egg mixture
  6. Coat the chicken generously in the breadcrumbs
  7. Heat oil over medium-high heat
  8. Remove chicken
  9. Transfer the chicken to a large, light colored baking sheet
  10. Top with marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese
  11. Bake uncovered for 12 minutes
  12. Garnish with fresh parsley
